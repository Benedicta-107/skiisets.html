About plantain porridge.
Plantain porridge is native to Niger Delta of Nigeria. It is mostly prepared with unriped plantain. It can also be prepared using riped plantain. To get the best result it is recommended to use unriped plantain.
Ingredients and measurement for preparing plantain porridge
Ingredients Measurement
Matured plantain 3
Ground crayfish 1 tablespoon
Palm oil 1 cooking spoon
Seasoning cube 1
Salt to taste 1/2 teaspoon
Ugu or Spinach or Lettuce 1 bunch
Onions 1 small bulb
Scotch bonnet 3 pieces
Smoked or fresh fish 1

Method of Preparation

  1. Wash, peel and cut the plantains into small sizes,
  2. Wash the Ugu/Spinach/Lettuce and slice into thin strips and set all aside for later use,
  3. Wash, peel, and chop the onions,
  4. Wash, chop or grind the pepper,
  5. Wash and clean the fish,
  6. Place the plantains into a pot, add enough water to slightly cover the plantains, the palm oil. Then add the chopped onions, the fish, salt, ground crayfish, pepper and seasoning cube,
  7. Leave to cook until the plantains are tender,
  8. Finally , add the sliced vegetables, and simmer covered for 3 minutes. Stir thoroughly, serve and enjoy.
